I've Been Everywhere - Johnny Cash Travel Tracker & Map

Welcome to the ultimate interactive travel tracker and checklist based on Johnny Cash's legendary country song, "I've Been Everywhere". If you love the music of the Man in Black and enjoy traveling across North America, South America, and the Caribbean, this is the checklist for you.

About the Song and the Project

Originally written by Australian singer Geoff Mack in 1959, and popularized in the Americas by Hank Snow, "I've Been Everywhere" became one of Johnny Cash's signature performances on his 1996 album Unchained. The song lists 92 distinct locations in a rapid-fire, rhythmic sequence.

Our interactive web application allows you to map your own travel history against the locations mentioned in the song. Register an account, log in, mark where you've been, and watch the map highlight the cities, states, and countries. See if you can go from "You've Been Nowhere" to "You've Been Somewhere" and finally reach 100% to claim "You've Been Everywhere!"
